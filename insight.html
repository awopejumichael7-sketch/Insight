School Policies

Discipline Policy

Insight School maintains a disciplined environment conducive to learning and personal development. Our discipline policy is based on respect, responsibility, and restoration.

Code of Conduct

  • All students must show respect to teachers, staff, and fellow students
  • Uniform must be worn correctly and completely at all times
  • Punctuality to school and classes is mandatory
  • Electronic devices are only permitted for educational purposes with teacher approval
  • Bullying, harassment, or violence of any kind will not be tolerated

Disciplinary Procedures

  1. Verbal warning for minor infractions
  2. Written warning and parent notification
  3. Meeting with parents/guardians
  4. Internal suspension for serious offenses
  5. Recommendation for expulsion for repeated or severe violations

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is essential for academic success. Insight School expects all students to maintain excellent attendance records.

Attendance Requirements

  • Students must arrive by 7:30 AM each school day
  • Morning assembly is compulsory for all students
  • Attendance is taken at the beginning of each class period
  • A minimum of 80% attendance is required to sit for termly examinations

Absence Procedures

  1. Parents must notify the school by phone or written note for any absence
  2. Medical certificates are required for absences exceeding 3 days
  3. Make-up work is the student's responsibility for missed classes
  4. Excessive absences will result in parent conference and possible retention

ICT Policy

Insight School provides technology resources to enhance learning while ensuring safe and responsible use.

Acceptable Use Policy

  • School computers and networks are for educational purposes only
  • Students must not attempt to bypass security or access restricted content
  • Personal devices may only be used with teacher permission during designated times
  • All users must respect copyright and intellectual property rights
  • Cyberbullying or inappropriate online behavior is strictly prohibited

Digital Citizenship

  1. Use appropriate language in all digital communications
  2. Protect personal information and passwords
  3. Cite sources properly when using online materials
  4. Report any security concerns or inappropriate content to teachers immediately

Examination Policy

Insight School maintains rigorous academic standards through fair and transparent examination procedures.

Examination Rules

  • Students must arrive at least 15 minutes before examination start time
  • Only permitted materials may be brought into the examination hall
  • Communication between students during examinations is prohibited
  • Electronic devices must be switched off and placed in bags
  • Any form of academic dishonesty will result in serious consequences

Grading System

  1. Continuous Assessment: 40% (Tests, assignments, projects, class participation)
  2. Termly Examinations: 60%
  3. Grading Scale: A (70-100%), B (60-69%), C (50-59%), D (45-49%), E (40-44%), F (Below 40%)
  4. Promotion requires a minimum average of 50% across all subjects
